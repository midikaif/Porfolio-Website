About Me

I'm Md Kaif Khan, a tech enthusiast and a hard-working web developer.

My interest in web development began after I took the O'level course from the prestigious National Institute of Electronics & Information Technology (NIELIT). In the course, I learned the basics of HTML and CSS and was introduced to my first programming language, which was C.

Currently, I'm enrolled in a course from Relevel by Unacademy, which focuses on advanced concepts in software development. Along with that, I have a good understanding of data structures and algorithms and I'm proficient in problem-solving.

Overall, I'm passionate about technology and enjoy keeping up-to-date with the latest trends and developments in the field. I'm always eager to learn new things and take on challenging projects that push me to grow as a developer.
